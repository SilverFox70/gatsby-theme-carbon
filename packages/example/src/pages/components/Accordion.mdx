<PageDescription>

The Accordion feature is comprised of three components - **AccordionGroup**, **Accordion**, and **AccordionItem**. The AccordionGroup is wrapper for a collection of Accordion components, providing margins and an optional group title. The Accordion component is a wrapper for AccordionItems where each item has a title and content. An AccordionGroup can be used inside of a `<Row>` and `<Column>`. 

</PageDescription>

<AccordionGroup title="Accordion Example">

</AccordionItem>

</AccordionItem>
<AccordionItem title="Third Item">

</AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

```

<Accordion>
<AccordionItem title="First Accordion Item">

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. Ut enim ad minim veniam, quis nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris nisi ut aliquip ex ea commodo consequat.

</AccordionItem>
```
